About this church

oboch main is an adventist church located in kenya kisumu county under the central nyanza confrence. This church was started in 2000 from the parent church Keyo SDA church due to large number of believers whofo Where travelling for a long distance to go for worship services and due to many opinion saying yes for the establishment of this church it was approved that it was the right decisions for the establishment of oboch main sda church.
